## 🏆 Progression System

### How to Advance:
1. ✅ Complete all lessons and read thoroughly
2. ✅ Complete all exercises (understand solutions)
3. ✅ Pass the level exam with **70% or higher**
4. ✅ Move to the next level

### What Happens if You Don't Pass:
- ❌ Review the grading rubric to see what you missed
- ❌ Go back to specific lessons
- ❌ Redo relevant exercises
- ❌ Retake the exam (unlimited attempts)
- ✅ Once you score 70%+, you unlock the next level
